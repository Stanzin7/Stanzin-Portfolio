Challenges Faced !

I pushed several patches this week but it was showing the error showed in below image. I surfed through many platforms like reddit, stack overflow, but socalled "WhiteSpace" error was not able to solve. My mentor saw many patches were correct, but they were not running the tests. He suggested me to correct EOL version to "Unix (LF)" & Encoding to "UTF-8". And boom, I got my patches running correctly.
